What is Tessr?

Tessr is an open-source distributed blockchain protocol.

While existing blockchain solutions offer a virtual asset to trade, our team is building a unified protocol to provide interoperability between blockchains. It will include easy coss-chain cryptocurrency payments integration, liquidity, and even a digital arbitration system.

In the end, Our goal is to integrate all blockchains into a unified blockchain ecosystem, which will make business truly efficient, transparent, and secure.

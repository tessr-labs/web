Developing the Blockchain OSI Reference Model

While existing blockchain solutions offer a virtual asset to trade, our team is building a unified protocol to provide interoperability between blockchains. It will include easy cross-chain cryptocurrency payments integration, liquidity, and even a digital arbitration system.

What is the Tessr Foundation?

A collective branching out to as many learning facilities as possible, to educate people of all ages. By doing so we can effectively involve the whole world with the development of blockchain technologies or to improve existing ones.

Our motive is to empower people worldwide to use and develop free and open source ideas. We will break the monopolization by teaching the youth and adults alike how share and create together!

Each user can be a developer or contribute their ideas, and with our bounty system, users will be paid for these contributions. The more we can enlighten the public and our youth of this technology, the more contributers our world will have.

    Join us in changing the world

The Tessr Wallet Wallet

Our Tessr wallet, allows you to manage all of your assets and wallets at the same time, secured by our Bio Key Ring technology.

With our patented Bio Key Ring technology, that only unlocks with your signature, anyone with a smartphone and an Internet connection can use any cryptocurrency anywhere in the world. This is done via our Tessr credit system, converting to the fiat currency of your choice instantly.

  • Micro transactions
  • Instant conversion to fiat currency
  • Secured with your Bio signature
  • No more expensive exchange fees
